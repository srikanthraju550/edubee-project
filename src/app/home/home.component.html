About Us


Who

The Engineers Factory is a socio-tech startup which objects to reform the Engineering Education System, formed by team of IIT Alumni and Industrial Experts with mission of bridge the gap between industry and academia.

Why

When there is a need for every gadget or application updating according to the contemporary trend, then why & how the academic curriculum almost remains the same. Helping students imbibe practical knowledge, making them mindful of the trends being set in the industry and Building a better nation where academia works in synergy with industry.

How

Get the experts insight, think like a professional, know the latest industry trends and interact directly with the professionals through The Engineers Factory innovations Tech Bank, Stuvations, Tech Connect ,Engship and many more.
